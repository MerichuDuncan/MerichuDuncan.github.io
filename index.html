STORY OF GLORY

The CODER

I'm originally from the Philippines, but have lived in Cleveland OH for ten years. It's been quite an amazing journey as well as a cultural adjustment. Through all the changes and challenges, one of the greatest constants I've had is an avid desire to learn and read about technology. I still remember the purchase of my first lap top an Ipad as well as the thrill of wondering how they worked. It was an interesting time in my life because I'd studied Hotel & Restaurant Management in college. I'd always loved the Hospitality Industry. in the Philippines it's one of the largest revenue generators in the economy. When I arrived in the United States, I'd always planned on using what I'd learned to become a general manager of a hotel one day. As time passed, however, my interests slowly shifted from Hospitality to Technology.

As all of this happened, I continued to enjoy life. I got married and my husband and I spent time traveling around the county. We went to San Antonio, Chicago, Los Angeles, & Los Vegas. Of all my travels, I enjoyed Zion National Park in Utah the most (I'm a big nature person). By the end of 2016, I'd finally committed to making a career change. I was reading a lot of articles on coding and found myself enthralled with industry. When I learned of an accelerated coding boot camp called "We Can Code It", I decided not to pass up the opportunity. In some ways, I took a leap of faith. I've worked in the hospitality industry for eight years, after all. With that being said, there was never any doubt that I'd succeed in school. I work hard. I do that because I know that success is not a guarantee and because I've always desired to be the best at what I do. That's been an especially useful skill the last several months as I learned the basics of software development. Hire Me Download Resume
